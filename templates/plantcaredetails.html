How to Maintain Different Types of Plants

Indoor Plants

Indoor plants thrive in indirect sunlight and require moderate watering. Avoid overwatering, and ensure proper air circulation.

  • Water every 7-10 days
  • Use well-draining soil
  • Wipe leaves occasionally

Outdoor Plants

Outdoor plants need direct sunlight and more frequent watering, especially in summer. Protect them from harsh winds and pests.

  • Water every 3-4 days
  • Fertilize monthly
  • Provide adequate sunlight

Succulents

Succulents require minimal water and plenty of bright, indirect light. They are sensitive to overwatering, which can cause root rot.

  • Water every 2-3 weeks
  • Use cactus mix soil
  • Ensure good drainage

Flowering Plants

Flowering plants need regular pruning, balanced sunlight, and frequent watering. Deadhead spent flowers to encourage new blooms.

  • Water every 4-5 days
  • Prune regularly
  • Use blooming fertilizer

Herbs

Herbs require well-drained soil, regular harvesting, and moderate watering. Most herbs prefer full sunlight for at least 6 hours a day.

  • Water every 2-3 days
  • Harvest often to promote growth
  • Use organic soil
